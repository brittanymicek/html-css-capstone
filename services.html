About

Puzzles, puzzles! Enigma is creates products designed for downtime, emphasizing good design, while winking to the unexpected, sparking curiosity, and making the minutes melt away.

Puzzles

We’ve art directed each puzzle to be challenging, completed at a slow burn. Irregular pieces allow for expanded play. Each puzzle piece features a soft-matte finish, so there’s no glare to irritate your eyes, even as the hours grow long. Our puzzles double as artwork you’d be proud to hang on your wall, or give a prime spot on your shelf.
